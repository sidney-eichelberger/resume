I recieved a double major in Biochemistry and Philosophy with a minor in Mathematics from Southern Methodist University in December of 2020. In September of 2021, I will begin my Ph.D. in Chemical and Biomolecular Engineering at UC Irvine. My interests lay in the research and development of bio-inspired materials for applications in sustainability and medicine.
